About Me

Welcome to my porfolio! My name is Shannon and I'm a Medical Laboratory Scientist with aspirations to become a full-stack developer.

Attention to detail, focus, and dedication to quality results are skills I mastered in the lab setting the past seven years, working in both hospital and clinical settings.

These transferable skills have allowed me to create applications that not only meet my clients' criteria, but exceed their expectations when it comes to design and function.

As someone who works equally well in the team setting and individually, I will be a valuable asset as we take on tough challenges and overcome obstacles together.

Please take a look around at my work and if it would be mutually beneficial to discuss my qualifications, reach out to me via the contact information below.

Thank you, and happy coding!
